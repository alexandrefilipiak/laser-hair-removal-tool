## Recommended Outreach Order

1. **Week 1-2: Transgender resources** (transgendermap, andreajames, deepstealth)
   - Warm targets, hair removal is critical to audience
   - Consumer protection framing resonates

2. **Week 2-3: Electrolysis clinics**
   - They WANT to differentiate from fake "laser" (IPL) clinics
   - Easy win - your tool supports their business

3. **Week 3-4: Industry publications** (dermascope, thebeautybrains)
   - Highest authority potential
   - Requires polished pitch

4. **Week 4-5: Training institutions**
   - Curriculum integration opportunity
   - Longer sales cycle

5. **Ongoing: Broken link replacement**
   - Systematic outreach to sites linking to dead blogs
   - Time-intensive but compounds over time
